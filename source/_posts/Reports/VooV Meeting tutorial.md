## 4. Join a meeting

### Method 1: Meeting ID

1) Click "Join" button
2) Input meeting id
3) Input your name
4) Click "Next" button

![image.png|555](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/qiyun71/Blog_images/main/MyBlogPic/202403/20250831164107.png)
