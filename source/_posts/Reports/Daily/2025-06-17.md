1.(关于Info-gap理论的实际应用挑战)
讲座中提到了Info-gap理论在工程应用中的优势，但在实际推广和应用中，特别是在非传统工程领域，其面临的主要挑战和限制是什么？ 
The lecture highlighted the advantages of Info-gap theory in engineering applications. However, what are the main challenges and limitations it faces in practical promotion and application, especially in non-traditional engineering fields?

2.(鲁棒性满意化与传统优化方法的结合) 
鲁棒性满意化强调“可接受”而非“最优”，在实际工程中，是否存在将鲁棒性满意化与传统优化方法结合的混合策略，以在保证鲁棒性的同时，尽可能地提升性能？ 
Robust satisficing emphasizes "acceptable" rather than "optimal." In practical engineering, are there hybrid strategies that combine robust satisficing with traditional optimization methods to maximize performance while ensuring robustness?

3.(认知不确定性量化的挑战) 
讲座强调了认知不确定性的重要性，但在实际操作中，如何更有效地识别、表征和量化这些“缺乏知识”引起的不确定性？是否有新的工具或方法正在发展中？ 
The lecture emphasized the importance of epistemic uncertainty. However, in practice, how can these uncertainties caused by "lack of knowledge" be more effectively identified, characterized, and quantified? Are there new tools or methods under development?

4.(Info-gap理论在AI/ML模型验证中的应用前景) 
随着人工智能和机器学习模型在工程领域的广泛应用，这些模型也存在不确定性。Info-gap鲁棒性分析是否可以应用于AI/ML模型的验证和鲁棒性评估？如果可以，有哪些独特的挑战？ 
With the widespread application of AI and machine learning models in engineering, these models also exhibit uncertainties. Can Info-gap robustness analysis be applied to the validation and robustness assessment of AI/ML models? If so, what are the unique challenges?

5.(鲁棒性与创新之间的平衡) 
在追求鲁棒性的同时，是否会限制工程设计中的创新空间？如何在确保系统鲁棒性的前提下，鼓励和实现突破性的设计？ 
Does the pursuit of robustness limit the scope for innovation in engineering design? How can groundbreaking designs be encouraged and achieved while ensuring system robustness?
