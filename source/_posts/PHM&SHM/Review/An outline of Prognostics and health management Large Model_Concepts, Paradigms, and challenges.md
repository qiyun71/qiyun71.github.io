## Background

Challenge in PHM:
- Scenario1
    - “the lack of generalization in PHM solutions and algorithms & models” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 8](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=8&annotation=8YC8A5PN))
    - “low utilization rate of field knowledge” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 8](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=8&annotation=K8NP5TSH))
    - “insufficient optimization of the PHM system.” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 8](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=8&annotation=HREZXZXJ))
- Scenario2
    - “the difficulty in conducting validations due to a lack of data and knowledge” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 9](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=9&annotation=YKU549WM))
    - “the poor interpretability resulting from the extensive use of black-box models” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 9](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=9&annotation=B8DPCI5W))

Ability of LM:
- “Long sequence modeling capability” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 15](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=15&annotation=BABLST4K)) **for modeling the degradation trajectory of equipment throughout its entire lifecycle**
- “Multi modal understanding ability” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 15](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=15&annotation=E8QDBN87)) **multimodal industrial data fusion and state perception**
- “The collaboration and scheduling capabilities” ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 15](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=15&annotation=AFZSEYHA)) **intelligent construction and decision-making ability improvement of PHM.**

## Innovation

Given the multifaceted nature of PHM, these Large Model can be further categorized into:
1. **Object-oriented PHM-LM**, such as models for bearing PHM, gear PHM, motor PHM, and electronic product PHM; 
2. **Task-oriented PHM-LM**, like models for data generation, solution generation, and verification & validation; 
3. **Algorithm & model-oriented PHM-LM**, including models for fault diagnosis, fault prediction, and maintenance decision-making. ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 15](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=15&annotation=EJUVKSI3))

3 typical innovation paradigms of PHM-LM:
- Paradigm I: PHM Paradigm based on LLM 
- Paradigm II: Parallel Paradigm of LLM and PHM Model
- Paradigm III: PHM-LM Construction and its Applications ([Tao 等, 2025, p. 16](zotero://select/library/items/EA4UD3UT)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/GFXBIP9I?page=16&annotation=BTGE8YXQ))
