Damage identification methods：
- model-based methods
  - modal parameter-based methods：分析损伤前后模态参数的差别，来确定结构损伤的位置和程度
  - finite element model updating methods：通过修正有限元模型的参数，来使得仿真模型的预测数据与实测数据匹配
- data-driven methods
  - Signal processing-based methods
  - Artificial intelligence-based method
  - **Population-based methods**：对相似的结构共享和使用marker data

## Outlook

Damage identification methods：
- “Firstly **model-based methods** are widely utilized in bridge SHM; however, **they are primarily applied to numerical simulations or simplified models**, which struggle to address complex real-world environments.” ([Zhang 等, 2025, p. 17](zotero://select/library/items/BH5G4VG6)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/NJNGSKWU?page=17&annotation=G4ZM4TUE))
- “**data-driven** damage identification methods encounter the challenge of **acquiring comprehensive labeled damage data**.” ([Zhang 等, 2025, p. 17](zotero://select/library/items/BH5G4VG6)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/NJNGSKWU?page=17&annotation=RA7EHMSN))
- Future：
  - “it is important to study **population-based SHM methods** and **multisource, multi-layer coupling algorithms** to identify stable and accurate heterogeneous migration learning methods, as well as **efficient data training algorithms**” ([Zhang 等, 2025, p. 17](zotero://select/library/items/BH5G4VG6)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/NJNGSKWU?page=17&annotation=Z2IQBX59))
  - “Secondly, with **the advancement of IoT and edge computing** technologies, future bridge SHM systems are expected to enable **real-time monitoring and online damage identification**.” ([Zhang 等, 2025, p. 17](zotero://select/library/items/BH5G4VG6)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/NJNGSKWU?page=17&annotation=57ZAJ9J8))
  - “**interpretability** of AI models(black box nature)” ([Zhang 等, 2025, p. 18](zotero://select/library/items/BH5G4VG6)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/NJNGSKWU?page=18&annotation=967SK5T9))
